Eligibility

Defendants who meet the following criteria are eligible for participation in the RWRC:



  • Defendant is an adult;
  • Resides in the RWRC jurisdiction;
  • Diagnosed with a serious mental illness and/or a co-occurring substance use disorder and/or intellectual and developmental disability;
  • Committed a nonviolent offense;
  • Convicted of a misdemeanor;
  • Understands and agrees to participate in the RWRC.




*The defendant must have the ability to freely and voluntarily consent to participation

The prosecutor and the RWRC treatment team make final eligibility determinations, and must agree that the client is a candidate for participation in the program.
