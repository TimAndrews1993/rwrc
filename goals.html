Goals



  • Coordinate the delivery of mental health services with probation oversight;
  • Enhance the mental, emotional and physical health of participants;
  • Provide participants tools to manage their mental health and/or co-occurring substance use and/or intellectual and developmental disabilities;
  • Incorporate the principles of recovery into participants' everyday lives;
  • Assist participants with housing and employment;
  • Reduce mental health hospitalizations and emergency room visits;
  • Reduce incarcerations;
  • Reduce recidivism;
