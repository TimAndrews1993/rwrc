The Program

The Regional Wellness and Recovery Court program is divided into four phases, and generally takes from 15 to 24 months to complete. How quickly you move through each phase will be determined by how quickly you meet your objectives.

As a participant in the program, you will be required to participate in mental health and/or substance abuse treatment.

You will be required to have regular contact with members of the RWRC Treatment Team, including the Care Manager, Peer Support Specialist, Home Compliance Officer and Community Service Supervisor, and Probation Officer.

During these meetings you will review your progress in mental health and substance abuse treatment, the results of drug and alcohol testing, home visits, educational groups, and receive counseling, mentoring and support.

You will also be required to attend and perticipate in RWRC Review Hearings to discuss your progress in the program. Based on your progress in the program, you will be required to appear for Review Hearings more or less often.



There are many benefits to participating in the RWRC program, including:

  • Improved health and well being
  • Sobriety
  • An improved understanding of problems that may have contributed to your conviction
  • New skills that will help you manage your symptoms and strengthen your recovery now and in the future
  • A stronger support system that can help you continue to improve your health and wellness, and maintain your gains
  • You may receive a reduced sentence
  • You may avoid incarceration
