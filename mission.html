Mission Statement

The mission of the 27th District Court Regional Wellness and Recovery Court is to provide integrated treatment and recovery support services to adult defendants convicted of misdemeanor crimes who suffer from serious mental illness, developmental disabilities, and/or co-occurring disorders, in order to reduce incarceration and recidivism within the criminal justice system, to reduce the costs of and improving access to mental health treatment.
