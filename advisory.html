Advisory Board

The RWRC Planning and Advisory Board is comprised of judges, probation officers, prosecutors, and defense attorneys from each of the participating district courts. Also on the board is the RWRC Judge, who presides over the meetings. The RWRC Care Manager and The Guidance Center's Director of Adult Behavioral Health are also members of the committee and participate in the meetings which are held semi-annually.

The Planning and Advisory Committee is charged with designing the RWRC, overseeing its operation, and strategic planning.
